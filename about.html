Nirguna Import Export

THE ULTIMATE TRUST POINT

With great pleasure, we present "Nirguna Import Export" as a market name for the import-export of natural foods and handcrafted goods. Ayurvedic and herbal, agricultural, spice, arts & crafts, processed foods, fruits, and vegetables are just a few of the things we deal in.
The founders of Nirguna Import Export, Mr. Ajay S. Shevkari and Mrs. Gitanjali A. Shevkari, have about 10 to 15 years of experience working in this industry. We are pleased to announce that we use cutting-edge technology to create exceptional agricultural products that come directly from farmers and associated processing units or merchants. Because of the superior quality of our Natural foods, we are a formidable competitor in the nutritional and Natural markets. All of our goods have been created with the consumer's unique demands and requirements in mind, ensuring satisfaction that goes above and beyond expectations. Our herbal and ayurvedic products are sourced from connected manufacturers and trading partners as well as Maharashtra (India) Van Dhan Vikas Kendras and the Forest Department.
